ABOUT US

Who We Are

Poppik Dropshipping is a zero-investment e-commerce solution designed to help individuals start their own Beauty, Lifestyle, and Wellness brand without any operational burden.

Our Goals

To empower aspiring entrepreneurs to start and scale their business with zero risk, zero stock, and zero hassle. You bring your brand name and domain.

Our Products

Our Values

How Poppik Dropshipping Works

1

Choose Your Brand Name & Domain

You start by selecting a brand name and purchasing a domain of your choice. No other investment is required.

2

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What investment do I need to start?

You only need to purchase your own domain. Website development, hosting, inventory, and fulfilment are completely managed by Poppik at zero cost.

Do I need to keep stock at home?

No. Poppik manages all inventory, packaging, and shipping. You don't need any storage or stock investment.

Who builds my e-commerce website?

Poppik builds your entire online store, including design, hosting, setup, and product uploads—completely free.

How do I track my orders and payments?

You get a dedicated dashboard where you can check new orders, delivered orders, payment status, and weekly settlements. Everything is fully transparent.

How does payment settlement work?

All payments made on your website are collected by Poppik. Every Monday, we settle your weekly earnings after deducting our margin.

What categories of products can I sell?

You can sell high-demand products in Beauty, Lifestyle, and Wellness. All curated and managed by Poppik.

Do I need technical knowledge?

No. Everything technical—website, hosting, backend, and fulfilment—is handled by Poppik. You just promote your brand.

Can I use my own brand name?

Yes. The website and business operate completely under your brand name and domain.
